The old doll

As I crawled into bed, I noticed a small, dark figure in the corner of my room. At first, I thought it was just my imagination, but as my eyes adjusted to the darkness, I realized it was a doll. It wasn't just any doll though, it was my childhood doll that I had lost years ago. I shook my head, trying to dispel the fear that was creeping up inside of me, but when I looked back, the doll was gone. Suddenly, I felt tiny hands wrap around my ankle and I knew I wasn't alone in the room.

    In this assignment I've gathered and editted some images and audio files. These are the steps I took:

  1. Looked for a short scary story which is suitable for the time limit of this assignment.
  2. Using a drawing AI generated lots of images and picked the most proper ones.
  3. Recorded My voice with Audacity application and adjusted the volume.
  4. Looked for royalty-free background audios and sound effects and chose the best matches.
  5. Created title and reference page using Canva.com.
  6. Transferred all of the files I previously gathered to OPS
  7. Added the background sound and set the volume to 10%. The background sound starts from the very beginning and is played in the entire video.
  8. Added the speech and soundeffects. I then sliced the speech because I wanted to use just sound effects in some parts. For instance, in one part of the video we can only hear frightened breathing. I did this to make the video a bit more scary.
  9. Added title page, images, and reference page and positioned them according to the audio. I also added some effects to images in some parts to make them more spooky.
